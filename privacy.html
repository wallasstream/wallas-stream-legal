5. Permissions Used

iPhone (iOS) may request:

    Used only to scan QR codes to establish a connection.
  • Bluetooth
    Used only to connect to compatible hearing devices or cochlear implants.
  • Local Network
    Used for direct audio transmission from your Mac.
  • Notifications (Optional)
    Used only to alert you when the connection is disconnected due to prolonged silence or inactivity. You can use the App without enabling notifications.

These permissions are never used for tracking or data collection.
